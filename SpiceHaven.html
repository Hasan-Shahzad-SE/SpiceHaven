Our Story

A Legacy Born
in the Kitchen

SpiceHaven began as a dream in a small home kitchen in Karachi — our founder Amna Raza wanting to share the warmth of her grandmother's recipes with the world. What started with a handful of tables has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Every dish on our menu tells a story. We source our spices fresh from local markets every morning, and our chefs have each trained for over a decade in the art of South Asian cooking.
